About Too Mikkii Tapas

Rooted in warmth, elevated through craft.

What began as a tree-lined cafe has grown into a culinary destination where every table feels like a celebration.

Our Story

A neighborhood heartbeat with global inspiration.

Too Mikkii Tapas started with one idea: create a place where Delhi slows down. We wanted guests to linger a little longer, talk a little deeper, and eat a lot better.

Today, our kitchen layers Mediterranean finesse, modern cafe comfort, and local freshness into every plate. The result is food that feels familiar and surprising at once.

From morning espresso rituals to late-evening gatherings under the trees, our story is written by the people who return for the feeling as much as the flavor.

"Every menu is a love letter to seasonality, texture, and shared joy."

Founder Note

Chef Aaryan Mehta

"Hospitality is the art of making someone feel remembered before they even ask."

Raised in a family where Sundays revolved around the table, Aaryan built Too Mikkii Tapas to honor both precision and emotion. His approach balances classical techniques with playful modern plating.

Aaryan Mehta

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our table.

2018

The first terrace tables

We opened as a 40-seat cafe focused on coffee rituals and small shared plates.

Guests discovered us for brunch, but stayed for sunset service and house signatures.

2020

Menu transformation

A chef-led menu debuted, pairing modern tapas ideas with seasonal Indian produce.

Our kitchen shifted from cafe staples to a full storytelling menu experience.

2023

Recognized destination

Too Mikkii Tapas became known as one of Delhi's most loved tree-lined dining spots.

Today, we continue refining the experience with every season, service, and signature plate.
